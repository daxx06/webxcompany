Reels & Short Video Production

Attention is the new currency. We write, edit, and produce highly engaging vertical video assets that make users stop scrolling and remember your brand.

The 3-Second Hook Rule

Every single day, users scroll past hundreds of feed posts and videos. If your video does not hook them in the first 3 seconds, they swipe away, and your content is buried by algorithms. Standard corporate promotion videos no longer work on modern social media platforms.

WebX builds video concepts optimized for high engagement retention. We write custom hooks, design dynamic caption text styles, select trending audio templates, and implement seamless zoom cuts and animations. This structure feeds the platform algorithms, driving high organic reach.

Complete Scripting & Guidance

Most local business owners want to post videos but struggle to write scripts or feel uncomfortable speaking on camera. We solve this. We write the exact word-for-word templates, show you how to shoot, and do all the post-production editing work to make you look like an industry expert.
