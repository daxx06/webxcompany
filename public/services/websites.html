Website Design & Development

A fast loading website is no longer optional. We build high-speed digital footprints designed mobile-first and optimized for local Google search visibility.

Why Page Speed Matters

Every second your site takes to load, you lose 20% of your potential customers. Standard drag-and-drop website builders are bloated with unnecessary JavaScript and heavy templates that slow down your server responses. This causes customers to hit the back button and go to your competitors instead.

WebX builds customized, clean-code static platforms. We remove the bloat, optimize image formats to modern standards, and host pages on global Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) for maximum performance. This guarantees your site loads in under a second and scores highly on Core Web Vitals.

Built-In Local SEO

A beautiful website is useless if no one in your neighborhood can find it. We integrate local search engine optimization directly into the code. This includes semantic HTML tag layouts, keyword-mapped headers, structured schema markups, and metadata configurations tailored for West Delhi markets.

  • Mobile-first responsive design for clinic/salon owners
  • Semantic heading structures (H1, H2, H3 tags)
  • Embedded maps and verified location coordinates
  • LocalBusiness JSON-LD markup included out of the box
