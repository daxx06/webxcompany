WhatsApp Chatbots & Automation

Reply at 2am without you being awake. We deploy official WhatsApp Business API assistant bots to capture leads, answer FAQs, and log bookings automatically.

Capture Leads Instantly

Local businesses run ad campaigns but lose leads because they take too long to reply to customer DMs. If a lead has to wait 2 hours for a response, they have already moved on to your competitor. Instant engagement is the key to local market conversions.

Our custom WhatsApp bots respond within 3 seconds of a user clicking your ad. The bot automatically presents a menu of services, answers frequent price questions, and logs the customer's phone and name. This keeps the lead warm and increases conversions by up to 40%.

Official compliance, No bans

Many developers pitch cheap WhatsApp bots using unofficial browser extensions or scraped web gateways. These unofficial APIs violate Meta's terms and will trigger permanent phone number bans, locking you out of your client lists.

WebX sets up official WhatsApp Business Cloud APIs through authorized Business Solution Providers (BSPs like AiSensy or Interakt). This guarantees compliance, enables green badge verification eligibility, and ensures high message deliverability rates.

Case Study

The Nail Studio

A West Delhi based luxury salon was spending hours replying to basic WhatsApp queries like "What is your pricing?" and "Are you open on Sunday?". This manual task was taking up their receptionist's entire time, leading to missed bookings.

WebX designed and deployed an official WhatsApp booking bot linked to a digital calendar. Customers can now click a link on Google Maps, instantly view open slots in WhatsApp, select a time, and confirm their booking. The bot automatically sends a reminder 2 hours before the slot, reducing no-shows to near-zero.
