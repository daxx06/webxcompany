Google & Meta Ads

Stop throwing money at impressions and clicks. We run outcome-focused local ad campaigns measured in direct customer inquiries and phone calls.

No "Agency Speak", Just Results

Many digital agencies hide behind complex reports filled with vanity metrics like impressions, reach, and click-through rates. They spend your hard-earned money showing your ads to people who aren't ready to buy, draining your marketing budget without generating any actual revenue.

At WebX, we focus exclusively on high-intent conversion actions. For local service businesses, this means phone calls, WhatsApp leads, and map direction requests. We set up precise conversion tracking metrics so you see exactly where every single rupee is spent and how many leads it brings in.

Hyper-Local Targeting

For businesses like dental clinics, academies, or beauty salons, your customers live nearby. We build hyper-targeted campaigns that show ads exclusively to people within a 3–5 kilometer radius of your storefront. This prevents budget waste on audiences too far away to visit.

  • Google Local Search ads pointing to Google Maps listings
  • Meta Lead Form ads built specifically for mobile screens
  • Customized negative keyword lists to filter non-buying searches
  • A/B testing of creative visual elements and headlines
